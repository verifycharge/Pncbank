We do not recognize the device you are using to sign in or you requested to receive a one-time time passcode every time you sign in. To help verify your identity, we need to send you a text message* or have you receive a phone call with a one-time passcode.

Step 2: Select how you would like to receive it:


Step 3: Click Send Passcode.

By clicking the Send Passcode button, you authorize PNC to either send you a text message or call you at the phone number selected to provide you with a one-time passcode.

* PNC does not charge a fee for using text message services. However, third party message and data rates may apply. These include fees your wireless carrier may charge you for data usage and text messaging services. Check with your wireless carrier for details regarding your specific wireless plan and any data usage or text messaging charges that may apply. Only valid U.S. mobile phone numbers associated with a U.S. mobile device are eligible to receive text messages from PNC.

For help with PNC one-time passcode text messages, text "HELP" to 76200. You may cancel these text messages from PNC at any time by texting "STOP" to 76200.

PNC does not charge a fee to call a non-U.S. phone number, but your telephone carrier may charge a fee. Check with your telephone carrier for more details.
